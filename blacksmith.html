BLACKSMITH

Forge of Creation

Each piece is born out of an intuitive process. Beginning with a sketch, Andreas delves into the world of blacksmithing to breathe life into his creations. The piece evolves organically, embodying the unconscious aspects developed through his unique talent and methodology.

A Mark of Excellence

Andreas’s exceptional work has earned him the prestigious Bonarota Prize of Art. This recognition is a testament to his artistic prowess and dedication.
