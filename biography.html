Meet Andreas Santis

Fusing art and metal, Andreas Santis is an acclaimed blacksmith artist. Having honed his craft since 2008, Andreas's expertise in sculpture and metalwork is unparalleled. With a Bachelor's degree in Fine Arts from the UK, he has further expanded his skills through specialized blacksmith workshops abroad.

Explore the Artistry

From imaginative sculptures to elegant furniture, Andreas's work spans a wide range of artistic expressions. Employing a semi-abstract style, his pieces are individualistic and laden with character. Browse through an array of creations wrought from iron, stainless steel, bronze, wood, epoxy, wax, and found scrap metal.
